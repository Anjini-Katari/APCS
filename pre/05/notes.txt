Relational Operators
 - output boolean datatypes 
 - ==, !=, >, <, >=, <=
 
Logic Operators (for logic statements)
 - && -> and
 - || -> or
 - ! -> not

De Morgan's Laws
 - !(A && B) is the same as !A || !B
 - !(A || B) is the same as !A $$ !B
